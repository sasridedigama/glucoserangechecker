Whats accuracy range?

The Dexcom G6 reading and your fingerstick meter value are unlikely to be exactly the same number, but they should be close.

If your G6 reading is within the meter range determined by the 30/30 rule, they match closely and the sensor is considered accurate.

30/30 Rule
The Dexcom G6 reading must be within: 30% above or below the meter value when the meter value is 80 mg/dL or higher 30 mg/dL above or below the meter value when the meter value is under 80 mg/dL
